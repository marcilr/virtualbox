VirtualBox


VirtualBox is a powerful x86 and AMD64/Intel64 virtualization 
product for enterprise as well as home use.  Not only is VirtualBox 
an extremely feature rich, high performance product for enterprise 
customers, it is also the only professional solution that is freely 
available as Open Source Software under the terms of the GNU General 
Public License (GPL) version 2. See "About VirtualBox" for an 
introduction.
--virtualbox.org/


"Oracle VM VirtualBox is an x86 virtualization software package, 
originally created by software company innotek GmbH, purchased by 
Sun Microsystems, and now developed by Oracle Corporation as part 
of its family of virtualization products. It is installed on an 
existing host operating system; within this application, additional 
guest operating systems, each known as a Guest OS, can be loaded 
and run, each with its own virtual environment."

How can I get VirtualBox to run at 1366x768?
Some good hints here:
o ...giving the VM 128MB of video memory.
o While the VM is running, you can also tell the guest OS
  to change the resolution by using:
  VBoxManage controlvm "Name of VM" setvideomodehint 1366 768 32
o If you go into your virtual machine's Settings > Display,
  and uncheck "Enable 3D Acceleration", then the guest will
  successfully run at 1366x768

Oracle VM VirtualBox (formerly Sun VirtualBox,
Sun xVM VirtualBox and Innotek VirtualBox) is
a free and open-source hosted hypervisor for
x86 computers and is under development by
Oracle Corporation. Developed initially by
Innotek GmbH, it was acquired by Sun Microsystems
in 2008, which was, in turn, acquired by Oracle in 2010.

VirtualBox may be installed on a number of host
operating systems, including Linux, macOS, Windows,
Solaris and OpenSolaris. There are also ports to
FreeBSD[3] and Genode.[4]
